Goodbye Glacier Ride

A 1.500 km cycling trip from Munich to Monaco with more than 30.000 altimeters. Passing the massive glaciers of the Alps and crossing its most spectacular cols. A two week cycling adventure to enjoy the beauty of the Alps and face the harsh reality of climate change.

Why

Glaciers are shrinking at record speed due to climate change.

Billions of people are dependent on glaciers for water and food.


De Schlatenkees in ca. 1920-1940 (links) en 2022. Bron 1920: Österreichische Nationalbibliothek, foto L 51918-B POR MAG. via glacierchange.com

We have to stop treating our atmosphere as a sewer and start to remove CO2 to preserve our glaciers and secure a sustainable future for all.

Support

The Goodbye Glacier Ride is initiated in 2025. It is a ride to show the bitter reality of climate change, but more important to ignite action to do something about it.

This is where you come in! You can support the riders by ordering one or more of the limited edition postcards. They will be send from the location with a personal note. The revenue will go to XXX good causes.

Do you want to ride the Goodbye Glacier Ride yourself and start your fundraising campaign? You can register here.
