Goodbye Glacier Ride

A 1.500 km cycling trip from Munich to Monaco with more than 30.000 altimeters. Passing the massive glaciers of the Alps and crossing its most spectacular cols. A two week cycling adventure to enjoy the beauty of the Alps and face the harsh reality of climate change.

Why

Glaciers are essential to life on Earth. Billions of people dependent on them for water and food.

However, glaciers are retreating at an alarming rate due to climate change. This threatens ecosystems, water resources, and human communities. Melting glaciers contribute significantly to sea-level rise, posing risks to coastal communities and causing saltwater intrusion into freshwater supplies.

To keep our planet livable, we need to protect our glaciers and halt climate change.
